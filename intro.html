This visualization is a tool to compare several factors about various undergraduate colleges in the United States.

This tool will allow you to compare the following.

Which state has more number of undergraduate colleges, less number of undergraduate colleges? Basically, how the undergraduate colleges in the US are distributed across different states?

Compare factors such as In-state tuition fees, out-state tuition fees for different colleges.

What is the percentage of students receiving funding? What are the popular degrees in a college?

How much money do students of a particular college make after graduation?
